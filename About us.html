About us

This website is designed by a group of college students who hope to achieve an online travel agency/ management system that would help customers find and book trips to various destinations with ease and with utmost comfort without facing any problems such as going to various websites or physical stores to understand the destination better and know every detail that it has to offer at the best price with greatest customer satisfaction.

We look to have a loyal customer base thus we have provided a very friendly user interface for ease of traversing between different pages. We hope to get customer reviews and suggestions that would help us improve this software in the near future as all of us were beginners to web developing and continue to focus on learning new aspects in the IT sector.
